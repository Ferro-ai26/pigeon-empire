# Art Bible

## Direction

- Cozy, readable, gently absurd rooftop pigeon colony.
- 2D isometric / 2.5D presentation with clear silhouettes and visual layering.
- Placeholder assets first: project-owned SVGs, simple shapes, and flat colors.
- Warm rooftop neutrals, sky blues, crumb gold, twig brown, and shiny highlights.

## Rules

- Gameplay readability outranks decoration.
- Keep interactable objects distinct from scenery.
- Use consistent isometric scale, footprint, and anchor conventions.
- Do not introduce final commercial art during this mandate.

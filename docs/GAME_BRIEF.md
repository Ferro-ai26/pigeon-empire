# Pigeon Empire — Game Brief

Status: FINAL  
Owner: Kevin  
Engine: Godot 4 / GDScript

Pigeon Empire is a compact 2D isometric / 2.5D cozy colony-management game with light idle progression. The player begins with a few pigeons on an abandoned rooftop, gathers resources, builds a small colony, assigns simple jobs, automates production, completes objectives, and constructs the Grand Roost.

## Product pillars

- Cozy, humorous, slightly absurd, readable, and easy to learn.
- Placeholder-first, data-driven, modular, and reskinnable.
- One compact rooftop and one bounded vertical slice.

## Core loop

1. Gather Crumbs, Twigs, and Shinies.
2. Spend resources on buildings.
3. Increase population and production capacity.
4. Assign pigeons to Forager, Builder, and Courier or Collector roles.
5. Upgrade and automate production.
6. Complete objectives and construct the Grand Roost.

Initial buildings are Basic Nest, Crumb Depot, Twig Workshop, Shiny Collector, and Grand Roost.

## Vertical-slice definition of done

One compact isometric rooftop; camera pan/zoom; resource gathering and counters; small pigeon population; simple jobs; validated building placement; passive production and upgrades; objectives; save/load and autosave; placeholder sound/art; concise onboarding; Grand Roost completion; a browser build when supported; and no critical or progression-blocking issue.

At completion, set `VERTICAL_SLICE_COMPLETE` and stop adding features.

## Explicit exclusions

No combat, enemies, multiplayer, trading networks, transportation systems, disasters, large tech trees, procedural worlds, multiple districts/maps, complex citizen simulation, advanced pathfinding, dozens of buildings/currencies, prestige, monetization, Android publishing, Steam integration, mod support, final commercial art, or web management UI.
